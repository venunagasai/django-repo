About Abacus Insights

Our Mission

Abacus Insights is a mission-driven technology company dedicated to liberating healthcare data. We believe that better data leads to better decisions, improved patient outcomes, and a more efficient healthcare system.

What We Do

We provide a best-in-class data usability platform specifically designed for healthcare payers. Our platform ingests, validates, and manages data from disparate sources, creating a single, golden source of truth. By breaking down data silos, we empower our clients to unleash the full potential of their data for analytics, machine learning, and operational excellence.

Why We Exist

The US healthcare system is complex and data-rich, yet data is often trapped in legacy systems and proprietary formats. Abacus Insights solves this interoperability challenge, ensuring that data is secure, compliant (FHIR, HIPAA), and readily available for those who need it most.

Join the Revolution

Based in Boston, MA, we are a team of passionate innovators, engineers, and healthcare experts working together to transform the future of healthcare.
